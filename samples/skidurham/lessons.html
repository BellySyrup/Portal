Ski and Snowboard Lessons

There's hardly a better way to spend a bright, winter's day than out on a slope skiing or snowboarding. Whether you like taking it easy on the green runs or working on rodeo flips and rail slides in the terrain park, there's lots of fun to be had and we can get you started with some lessons.

Beginner and advanced students can take advantage of the many professional ski and snowboard instructors in Durham Region's many ski resorts. The current schedule for classes is posted here, and it will periodically updated.
